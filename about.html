About On The Block

On The Block documents the history and ongoing story of hip hop with a focus on Chicago drill — part encyclopedia, part live feed. We combine deep research with the voices and clips that shaped the culture.

Want to suggest a topic, add missing details, or correct something? Open a discussion or send us a note — community knowledge powers the archive.
