Complete History of Chicago Drill (2010–2020s)

Drill took root in Chicago’s South and West Sides during the early 2010s, powered by inexpensive cameras, YouTube uploads, and a generation narrating its reality. The sound fused stark melodies with punishing drums, and the visuals made block identities legible to the internet. The industry tried to ignore it — then tried to copy it. The rest is everywhere.
